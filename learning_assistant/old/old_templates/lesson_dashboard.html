Description

Welcome! This online course (work in progress) is designed to introduce you to Python programming.

It aims to provide you with a high-level understanding of foundational concepts in Python programming.

This course is exercise heavy! It is primarily tailored for those who learn by doing. You'll also have access to Code Companion, a personalized Tutor (powered by GPT-4), whom you can ask questions to when you need help. While it won't provide answers, it will offer valuable hints and steer you in the right direction.

Each lesson offers valuable links to key concepts at the top. If you prefer a lecture-based course, you may consider starting with this. You can always return here later to complete the exercises.

I hope this course can serve as a starting point on your journey into the world of computer science.
