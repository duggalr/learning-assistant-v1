📝 About

Companion is exploring how Generative AI models can effectively be utilized, to help foster a more personalized educational experience for individuals.

At the moment, Companion offers individuals 3 main benefits:

  • An interactive code editor environment, where you can run your code and ask the AI for guidance or feedback, on the problems you are working on.
  • A free Python course focused primarily on exercises, enabling you to learn or hone your skills. For additional Python practice, feel free to check out CodingBat or MIT OCW
  • A general tutor, where you can get help on any topic you are trying to learn. Examples include: getting help on an essay, learning a new programming question, or getting help on a specific homework problem.
