Companion

📝 About

Companion is exploring how Generative AI models can effectively be utilized, to help foster a new, more personalized educational experience for individuals.

At the moment, Companion offers the following benefits for individual learners:

  • An Online Code Editor, where you can run your code and ask Companion for guidance and feedback, as you work through your problems.
  • A Chat Interface, where you chat with Companion and get help or discuss any Computer Science concept you are interested in. You can also develop personalized step-by-step roadmaps for Computer Science concepts or skills you want to learn.

🔌 Get Involved

This solution is merely only the start, and will go through several iterations. If you are interested and want to get involved, please reach out.
