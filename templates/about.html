About:

File AI enables you to have conversations with GPT-4, on your personal files.

You also have the option of also generating a concise summary of your uploaded file, along with generating questions to help you get a deeper understanding of the information.

File AI also generates tags for each of your uploaded files, to help you organize and retrieve your information faster.

Potential use cases includes:

  • Ask specific questions about a content presented in the file, to help you consolidate & find information faster.
  • Ask GPT-4 to help implement the model a research paper proposed.
  • Ask GPT-4 to test your understanding of the concepts presented in a file.
  • Easier organization & retrieval of your files.

Potential Features down the line:

  • Adding support for greater file types: Excel, Text, Audio, Video.
  • Adding support for saving webpages.
  • Providing search and easy retrieval of one's documents.
  • Automatically developing a knowledge graph from unstructured data to enable more Q/A use cases.

Reach out if you have ideas, suggestions, questions, or want to help engineer File AI!

Frequently Asked Questions: Reach out if you have any specific questions or concerns.

  • Is my data private?
    • Here's our rule of thumb: Don't upload a file that you aren't comfortable with OpenAI using or seeing.

      We leverage OpenAI's API to enable the user to ask questions for their specific document. This obviously requires us to send parts of the file data to OpenAI's servers, where they will return the correct response.

      Don't upload files you feel are confidential. This is primarily meant to be a place for you to upload research papers, excel sheets, documents to enable you to get answers to your questions more quickly.

  • Why am I only limited to 10 files?
    • We will add more, this is just the beginning! Email us, and we will increase your threshold.
