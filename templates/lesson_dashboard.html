Description

Welcome! This online course is designed to introduce you to Python programming.

It aims to provide you with a high-level understanding of foundational concepts in Python programming, enabling you to confidently solve small, specific problems.

This course is entirely exercise-based, tailored for those who learn by doing. You'll also have access to a personalized Tutor (powered by GPT-4), who will guide you and review all your submissions. While it won't provide answers, it will offer valuable hints and steer you in the right direction.

Each lesson offers valuable links to key concepts at the top. If you prefer a lecture-based course, you may consider starting with this. You can always return here later to complete the exercises.

I hope this course can serve as a starting point on your journey into the world of computer science.
