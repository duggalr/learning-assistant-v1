About

We are exploring how Generative AI Models can be effectively utilized in the classroom to enhance and optimize the educational experience for both students and teachers.

Watch the Comprehensive Demo , where I go through all the core use cases of Code Companion, for students and teachers.

For Students:

At the moment, we have built Code Companion, a tool that offers students a playground code editor, where they can work on their problems, while being able to ask any questions they have to an AI-powered tutor.

The goal here is really to try to create learning a more personalized experience for each student, where they are able to understand the material at their own pace.

Students are also able to immediately get answers to their questions, along with feedback for each question they work on.

For Teachers:

Code Companion also helps teachers, eliminating a lot of the administrative headache that is part of the teaching profession.

With Code Companion, teachers will get help with creating their lesson plans, generating questions/quizzes, and get assistance with marking and providing student feedback.

Teachers will also be able to see the questions and conversations their students are having with the tutor, enabling them to get a better understanding of what concepts they should further review in class.
